My Projects

This project is a text-based, two-player board game inspired by The Lion King, where players take on the role of young lions competing to become the next Pride Leader. Each player selects a character with unique starting stats, chooses a path—Cub Training for long-term growth and mentorship or Straight to the Pride Lands for faster short-term gains. The journeys across a randomized 52-tile lane filled with regular and special events. Players spin a virtual spinner to move, face challenges like riddles, hyena ambushes, or oases.
