My Hobbies

Cycling

Ever since I moved to Boulder I have fallen in love with cycling. This bike is called a "fixed gear" bike — meaning the pedals and rear wheel are directly connected, so you can't coast. I scoured local marketplaces to source all the parts and was able to assemble it myself for under $150.

Keyboards

I love vintage electronics. The idea that we've progressed so fast that computers 20–40 years ago are now considered junk. I used to visit electronic waste facilities just to dig around for artifacts of the past. While most vintage PCs are unusable, the keyboards are usually salvageable.
